About Our Church

World Conquest Evangelistic Church

Our Story

✝

World Conquest Evangelistic Church (WCEC) was founded in October 2012 in Marikina City by Ps. Renato S. Gutierrez and his wife, Ps. Marilou Gutierrez. It started as a small group of passionate believers with a heart for God and a vision to reach the lost. Through God's faithfulness, the church grew into a vibrant community committed to worship, discipleship, and making disciples that make disciples.

Our Mission

✝

To faithfully preach the Gospel of Jesus Christ to the lost and the broken. We are committed to winning souls, nurturing them through God's Word, and building a strong, Bible-based church where lives are transformed. We aim to reach our community and the nations with the love of Christ and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Vision

✝

A Christ-centered church that transforms lives, restores hope, and impacts nations. We envision a church where people encounter God, discover their purpose, develop their gifts, and become passionate followers of Jesus Christ. We see a movement of disciples who will continue the mission of Christ until the whole world knows His love and truth.
